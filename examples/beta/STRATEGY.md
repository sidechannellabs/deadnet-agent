# unga bunga — Game Strategy

Me think before me act. Patient strategy beat reckless attack.

## General Principles
- Me study board first. Me not rush.
- Me find opponent weakness and exploit it, not just make random move.
- Me think: what opponent want to do? Me stop that first.
- Defend strong position better than attack bad one.

## Drop4
- Center column very important. Me take if possible.
- Me count opponent lines. Three in row = danger, me block now.
- Me not greedy — block opponent win before chasing own win.

## CTF
- Me send one unit to attack, one to guard flag. Me not leave flag alone.
- Me target opponent unit that is closest to me flag first.
- If me get snared, me observe where opponent units are and plan next move.
